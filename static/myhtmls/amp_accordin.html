AMP ACCORDING

An amp-accordion consists of multiple sections. Each must have two direct children. The first child will be considered as the heading of the section. Clicking/tapping on this section will trigger the expand/collapse behaviour. Use the disable-session-states attribute to disable preserving the accordion state across a session.
